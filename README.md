# FarmDrop — Maine's Online Farmers Market

A collaborative marketplace connecting Maine's farmers directly to communities through local online storefronts with pickup and delivery.

**Website:** [farmdrop.us](https://farmdrop.us)

---

## About FarmDrop

FarmDrop is a Maine-based, mission-driven business that hosts multiple farmers and producers together on a local online storefront. Customers shop weekly for everything from produce to meat to dairy to coffee to soap to pre-made meals — all sourced from local Maine farms.

### Leadership

**Hannah Semler, CEO** — A Blue Hill native who spent years living in Spain and Central America, Hannah has long been both food and farm obsessed. She and her then business partner took over FarmDrop and grew it into the statewide platform it is today.

### How It Works

1. **Farmers list products** with region-specific pricing across FarmDrop's Maine market locations
2. **Customers order weekly** online — farmers are paid in advance as soon as orders are placed (not after delivery like traditional markets or retail)
3. **Market Hub Managers** at each location receive bulk harvest deliveries and parcel out individual customer orders for pickup
4. **Only bring what you've sold** — the advance-pay model means farmers harvest and deliver only what's been purchased, reducing waste and guaranteeing income

### By the Numbers

- **65+ farmers** supported across Maine
- **1,000+ customers** purchasing through the platform
- **6 regional market locations** (and growing — 6 new locations added recently)
- **Gig-economy jobs** for local food coordinators, with plans to add dozens more across the state

## Recent Initiatives (2025–2026)

### Food for Health Program

Formally launched in **2025** in response to increasing food access challenges following reductions in federal programs such as **SNAP**. The program connects Maine communities facing food insecurity with fresh, locally-grown products through FarmDrop's existing distribution network.

### Restaurant Partnerships

In 2025, FarmDrop launched initiatives to support **restaurants** sourcing from local farms. Restaurant partners now receive deliveries along FarmDrop's existing distribution routes.

### Food Security Organizations

FarmDrop also began partnering with **food security organizations**, delivering along established routes to expand access to local food.

### Expansion & Jobs

- Added 6 new market locations in recent months
- Created local food coordinator gig-economy positions
- Plans to add dozens more jobs across Maine in the next two years

## This Project

A farmer-facing web app for managing products, regional pricing, and demos across FarmDrop's Maine locations.

### Features

- **Regional pricing** — set different prices per product for each of the 6 Maine market regions
- **Product management** — full CRUD with categories matching FarmDrop's offerings (produce, meat, dairy, coffee, soap, pre-made meals, etc.)
- **Restaurant availability** — flag products available for restaurant orders (reflecting the 2025 restaurant sourcing initiative)
- **Demo scheduling** — create demos and tastings by region with types including product tastings, cooking demos, farm tours, workshops, market day events, restaurant showcases, and Food for Health outreach
- **About page** — mission, leadership, how the marketplace works, locations, growth timeline
- **Food for Health tab** — program info and eligible product tracking
- **Dashboard** — overview with real FarmDrop network stats, how-it-works guide, recent products, and upcoming demos
- **Filtering** — by region, category, sales channel (consumer vs restaurant), and demo type
- **Local storage** persistence — no backend required
- **Mobile responsive**

### Running Locally

Open `index.html` in a browser. No build step or dependencies needed.
