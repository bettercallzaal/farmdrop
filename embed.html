Food for Health - Three Ways to Give

Formally launched in 2025 in response to SNAP reductions, Food for Health buys surplus from Maine farms and routes it through FarmDrop's existing delivery network to food security partner organizations across Downeast Maine. Three giving channels:

2% Auto-Reinvest

Every FarmDrop order you place automatically routes 2% to Food for Health. You don't have to do anything. You just have to shop.

Checkout Donation

Add any amount at checkout - it goes straight to Food for Health. Fastest way to feed a neighbor this week.

Buy-One-Donate-One

Select items priced so that buying one for yourself also funds one for a food-insecure neighbor through FFH's partner orgs.
