How FarmDrop Works

A farmers' market and a CSA had an online baby. That is FarmDrop.

1

Farmers List Products

Local producers list everything they grow or make — no shelf-space limits. Unit sizes from a pint of jam to a whole lamb.

2

Customers Order Weekly

Shop online through your regional FarmDrop. Farmers get paid the moment you check out, not after delivery.

3

Market Hub Pickup

Farmers deliver in bulk to a regional Market Hub Manager who packs individual orders. You pick up midweek at your location.

4

Zero Waste

Farmers only bring what is sold. Customers only get what is fresh-harvested that morning. Nothing spoils on shelves.

Food for Health

Formally launched in 2025 in response to SNAP reductions, Food for Health buys surplus from Maine farms and routes it through FarmDrop's existing delivery network to food security organizations across Downeast Maine. Three ways to give:

2% Auto-Reinvest

Every FarmDrop order automatically sends 2% to Food for Health. Shopping is the support.

Checkout Donation

Add any amount at checkout. Goes straight to FFH. Fastest way to feed a neighbor.

Buy One, Donate One

Select items priced so one purchase funds one for a food-insecure neighbor through FFH partner orgs.

Spring & Summer 2026 Events

Five community gatherings across Downeast Maine — panels, film screenings, the Soulbenders benefit, and a dance party for Food for Health.
